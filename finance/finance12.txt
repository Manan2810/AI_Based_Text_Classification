The field of personal finance focuses on individuals and households managing their financial resources effectively. It includes budgeting, saving, investing, retirement planning, and managing debt. Personal finance aims to enhance financial well-being and ensure individuals can meet their short-term and long-term financial goals.
The study of finance also encompasses various financial theories and models that help in understanding and predicting market behavior. The efficient market hypothesis, capital asset pricing model (CAPM), option pricing models, and portfolio theory are some examples of frameworks used by finance professionals to analyze and make informed decisions.
